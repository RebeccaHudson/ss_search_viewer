Plot Available

Indicates if composite logo plot is available or not (only composite logo plots with pval_rank<0.05 are available). By clicking “Show plot”, the page scrolls back up to the composite logo plot of the SNP-motif pair. atSNP database has all composite logo plots with a significance change on the matches at level 0.05. Composite logo plots of SNP-motif pairs with no significant changes at level 0.05 are not available on the web. User interested in plotting them can create plots with atSNP R package available at https://github.com/keleslab/atSNP. We provide two examples below to help users quickly understand how to read the plot.

Plots shown are in SVG format. In order to download a plot, right-click on the plot and click "Save Image As", or the equivalent on your browser.
